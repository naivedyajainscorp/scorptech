Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Scorptech Technologies Private Limited ("SCORP", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you interact with our website, products, and services, including Sapphire ("the application").

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal, organizational, and operational data through direct submissions, account registrations, usage logs, and communications. This may include names, contact details, device info, IP addresses, geolocation, asset details, and usage statistics.

2. Purpose of Data Collection
  • To deliver core functionalities of the Sapphire ERP
  • To personalize the experience and enhance productivity
  • To improve system performance and security
  • To comply with legal obligations and industry regulations
  • To send critical updates, releases, and transactional communications
3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell user data. Limited sharing may occur with service providers for essential operations, legal compliance, or as authorized by the user. Any third-party integrations (e.g., payment gateways, APIs) are bound by independent agreements and privacy standards.

4. Data Security

SCORP implements encryption, access controls, secure hosting, firewalls, and audit trails to protect user data. Despite best efforts, no method is 100% secure. Users are advised to maintain strong credentials and report any suspicious activity.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as required for contractual, operational, legal, and archival purposes. Users may request data deletion subject to compliance with statutory and regulatory obligations.

6. Children and Age Restrictions

Users must be 18 years or older to operate or manage Sapphire modules. Underage users may only access limited interfaces under supervisor control and only within the permissions allowed by their organization’s policy.

7. Your Rights
