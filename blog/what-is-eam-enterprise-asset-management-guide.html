The Common Thread: Every Indian Enterprise Has the Same Asset Problem

A Jaipur Factory, a Mumbai Hospital, a Chennai Refinery — Same Problem

Scale differs. Regulation differs. Budget differs. But every single Indian enterprise runs on physical assets — and loses money when those assets aren't managed strategically.

Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) isn't a nice-to-have. It's the difference between a machine running at 89% OEE versus costing ₹47 lakhs annually in lost production.
